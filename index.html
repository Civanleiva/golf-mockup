SANDALS EMERALD BAY GOLF COURSE

World-Class Golf in Great Exuma

Designed by gold legend Greg Norman, the award-winning Sandals Emerald Bay Golf Course is recognized as one
of the most scenic 18-hole ocean-side gold courses in the Caribbean and renowned for its challenging fairways. A
golfer's paradise, six signature holes hug the coastline of a scenic peninsula, and prevailing trade winds promise
new experiences with each game.

Championship Golf Course

by Legendary Pro-Golfer Greg Norman

Located at the Bahamas' Leading Golf Resort, Greg Norman's award-winning golf course design at Sandals
Emerald Bay preserves the landscape's pristine beauty and natural barriers. As the longest course in the
Caribbean, winding through seaside dunes along a stunning peninsula, this 18-hole, par 72 championship course is
consistently regarded as one of the best in the Caribbean by Travel + Leisure Golf. Sandals Emerald Bay Golf Course
is the proud host of the Bahamas Great Exuma Classic, a PGA TOUR-sanctioned Korn Ferry Tour event.
